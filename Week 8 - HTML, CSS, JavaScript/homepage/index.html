History of Automobiles

The modern automobile is an advanced machine comprising of many complex technical systems working together in an intricate ecosystem. Today's automibiles and their technology have come a long way since their humble beginnings.

Car brands

Today there are approximately 60 different car brands hailing from all corners of the globe. Some are titans of the automotive industry while some are bespoke manufacturers, some have a rich history while some are just burgeoning companies.

Types of cars

In today's market, there are approximately 400 car models on offer, with many different body shape, size and engine types. However, all cars today can be classified into 1 of 9 major categories, each with their unique characteristics.
